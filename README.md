# Product Dissection of Johnson & Johnson

## Project Overview
This project provides a detailed product dissection and business analysis of Johnson & Johnson (J&J), a global healthcare company known for its pharmaceuticals, medical devices, and consumer health products. The project explores how J&J solves real-world healthcare challenges through innovation, accessibility, and technology-driven healthcare solutions.

---

## About Johnson & Johnson
Founded in 1886, Johnson & Johnson is one of the world’s leading healthcare companies operating across:
- Pharmaceuticals
- Medical Devices
- Consumer Health Products

The company focuses on improving patient care, healthcare accessibility, safety, and innovation worldwide.

---

## Project Objectives
- Analyze J&J’s product ecosystem
- Understand real-world healthcare problems
- Study product-based business solutions

## Real-World Problems & Solutions

### Wound Care Accessibility
- Problem: Easy and safe wound treatment access
- Solution: Band-Aid products

### Safer Pain Management
- Problem: Opioid dependency risks
- Solution: Tylenol as a non-opioid alternative

### Vision Care Accessibility
- Problem: Affordable vision correction
- Solution: Acuvue contact lenses

### Infant Care Safety
- Problem: Safe skincare for infants
- Solution: Johnson’s Baby products

### Advanced Cancer Treatment
- Problem: Effective targeted therapies
- Solution: Darzalex treatment innovations
